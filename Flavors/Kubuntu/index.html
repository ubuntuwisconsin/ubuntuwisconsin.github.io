Name: Kubuntu

First release: 5.04

Initial release date: April 8, 2005

Desktop environment: KDE

Description:

Kubuntu gives Windows users an easy transition over to the world of Ubuntu/Linux, providing a familiar feel and a beautiful desktop.

Kubuntu is less resource-intensive then Ubuntu, but it isn't the best, either. KDE takes 512 MB of RAM, and on a 6-core machine with 8 GB of RAM runs smooth enough to get by. If you have a computer older then 4 or 5 years, then you probably shouldn't use this flavor.

System requirements:

Absolute minimum:

  • 768 MB of RAM
  • 1 GHz processor
  • 4 GB of hard drive space
  • VGA capable of 640x480 screen resolution
  • Either a CD/DVD drive or a USB port for the installer media

Recommended:

  • 3 GB of RAM
  • Dual core 1.5 GHz processor
  • 25 GB of hard drive space
  • Any display capable of 1280x1024 or more
  • Either a CD/DVD drive or a USB port for the installer media
  • Internet access
