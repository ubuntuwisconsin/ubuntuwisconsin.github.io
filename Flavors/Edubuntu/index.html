Name: Edubuntu

First release: 5.10

Initial release date: October 13, 2005

Desktop environment: Unity

Description:

Edubuntu is Ubuntu, but it comes with a lot of different applications meant to help school-age children learn. It is useful for education.

Since Edubuntu is the same as Ubuntu, all the recommendations are the same.

System requirements:

Absolute minimum:

  • 384 MB of RAM
  • 700 MHz processor (about Intel Celeron or better)
  • 5 GB of hard drive space
  • VGA capable of 1024x768 screen resolution
  • Either a CD/DVD drive or a USB port for the installer media

Recommended:

  • 4 GB of RAM
  • Dual core 1.5 GHz processor
  • 25 GB of hard drive space
  • Any display capable of 1280x1024 or more
  • Either a CD/DVD drive or a USB port for the installer media
  • Internet access
