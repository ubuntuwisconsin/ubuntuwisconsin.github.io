Download Ubuntu GNOME 15.10

Name: Ubuntu GNOME

First release: 13.04

Initial release date: April 25, 2013

Desktop environment: GNOME

Description:

Ubuntu GNOME provides a nice looking, functional desktop. It is very customizable and it fits together nice.

Ubuntu GNOME is more resource-intensive then Kubuntu, but less resource-intensive then Ubuntu. You will have to have a modern computer, but not necessarily a beefy computer.

System requirements:

Absolute minimum:

  • 1 GB of RAM
  • 1 GHz processor
  • 7 GB of hard drive space
  • VGA capable of 1024x768 screen resolution
  • Either a CD/DVD drive or a USB port for the installer media

Recommended:

  • 2 GB of RAM
  • Dual core 2 GHz processor
  • 30 GB of hard drive space
  • Any display capable of 1280x1024 or more
  • Either a CD/DVD drive or a USB port for the installer media
  • Internet access
